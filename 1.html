About us

you can find many popular species of animals in our zoo

The owners of the Eden's Reserve. conducted many wildlife rescues before they decided to create a reserve that is specifically set for their rescued animals, not only that the reserve also includes animals that have been domesticated since birth, therefore their offspring will be raised inside the enclosure under the owners careful protection, the owners decided to make a zoo out of it in order to earn profit that can support the lifestyle of the animals in the reserve.

Get to touch the animals

There are only a handful of animals the visitors are allowed to spend time with, even if the animals are almost raised within captivity by humans, the animals might still act wildly knowing that the animals don't know the visitors, and they aren't well equipped with handling such complicated yet majestic creatures. The animals that are available for human interactions are the zebra and the chameleon.
